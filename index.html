About

Originally from Cape Town, South Africa, Tawfeeq Martin has spent over twenty years at the frontier of creative technology — from pioneering real-time production tools at The Mill to engineering the LED volumes at ILM that changed filmmaking. Working on StageCraft was a childhood dream come true.

Beyond production stages, he explores generative art and is quietly building at the intersection of technology and faith. Recognized by Cannes Lions, SXSW, The One Show, and HPA. Member of the Visual Effects Society.
